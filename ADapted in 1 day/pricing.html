Frequently Asked Questions

How does the access code work? +
After purchasing a course, you'll receive a unique code. Share this code with your team members (up to 15). Each person enters the code to create their account and access the course.
Can I switch between courses? +
With the Monthly Subscription, you can switch between any online courses. One-time purchases are locked to the selected course.
How long does course access last? +
One-time purchases include 6 months of access. Subscriptions provide access as long as the subscription is active.
What if I need more than 15 seats? +
You can purchase multiple courses or subscriptions. Contact our sales team for volume discounts on larger teams.
